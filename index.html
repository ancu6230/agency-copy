Who we are

The Digital History Advanced Research Projects Accelerator (DHARPA) is an interdisciplinary team working to enable critical DH practice. We want to help scholars lift the lid on ‘black box’ technologies, to take ownership of their interventions, and to embrace their role in the process of knowledge production.

Software & Methodologies

User-friendly techniques and tools to operationalize self-reflexive, reproducible scholarship.

Collaboration

A responsive laboratory to rapidly prototype and launch applied solutions.

Outreach & Infrastructure

Training and platforms to help you start, sustain and maintain digital research projects.
